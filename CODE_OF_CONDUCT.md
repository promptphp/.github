# Code of Conduct

This project adopts the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/),
version 2.1, as its code of conduct.

We pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body
size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of
experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity
and orientation.

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include showing empathy and kindness, being
respectful of differing opinions, giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback, and focusing on what is
best for the community. Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, trolling, insulting or derogatory comments, and
publishing others' private information without permission.

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to the maintainers at
**promptphposs@gmail.com**. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.

The full text is available at
[contributor-covenant.org](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).
